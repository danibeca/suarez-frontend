    After all designs are approved and a down payment is made, turnaround time will be of 4 weeks. We use FedEx Courier. The estimated transit for the order when it leaves our premises is 4-5 business days.

  4. What are the payment options?
    You can make a full 100% payment to start the production by Credit Card or Bank Wire, also we have a Credit Card payment when you check out for your order when you are member of a team logged in our system.
